Welcome to the Mirror Site

This is a fan-made repository for Echoes of Ender. This mod is inspired by the weekly catch-ups of Mckenzie (MadJad_) and John (SiiN).

After hearing how the hosts met during a Minecraft Bedwars match back in 2019, I felt compelled to create a mod that brings their unique blend of pop culture, gaming expertise, and "that reminds me" anecdotes into the world of Minecraft.

The Origins

The Oh That Reminds Me podcast is about everything that makes us: movies, music, and the games we play. This mod tries to capture that spirit. Whether it's John's competitive edge from his Rainbow Six Siege days or Mckenzie's lifestyle and cosplay influence, there's a piece of the show in every line of code.

Core Mechanics (v2.4.0)

  • Bedwars Legacy: Enhanced bridge-building mechanics and defensive block buffs inspired by the 2019 match that started it all.
  • Siege Tactics: A "Leaning" mechanic (Q/E keys) that allows players to peek around corners, a tribute to John's R6 Siege background.
  • Pop Culture Echoes: Occasional world events themed after movies like Wicked, Stranger Things, and FNAF 2.
  • "The Tea" Scrolls: Craftable items that reveal lore about the "Endless Vox" world in the style of Mckenzie's storytelling.
  • Streamer Awards Trophy: A rare collectible that can be displayed in your base.

How to Install

  1. Install Forge (1.16+).
  2. Download the JAR file below.
  3. Drop the file into your mods folder.
  4. Launch and listen to the latest episode while you play!
